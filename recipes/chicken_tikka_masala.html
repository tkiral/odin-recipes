Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

This is a buttery, tasty Indian chicken dish that is very popular in the west. It goes well with naan and rice.

Ingredients

For the chicken marinade

  • 800g boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1.5 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon ginger
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon Kashmiri chili
  • 1 teaspoon salt

For the sauce

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 small onions
  • 1.5 tablespoons ginger
  • 1.5 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 400g tomato puree
  • 1 teaspoon Kashmiri chili
  • 1 teaspoon ground red chili
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1.25 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 0.25 cup water
  • 4 tablespoons fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Finely chop the onions
  2. Cut the chicken into bite-sized cubes
  3. Finely grate the ginger
  4. Combine the chicken with all of the ingrediants for the chicken marinade
  5. Let the marinade sit over night
  6. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat
  7. When the oil is sizzling, add the chicken pieces in batches, making sure not to crowd the pan
  8. Fry for about 3 minutes until browned on each side
  9. Set the chicken aside and keep it warm
  10. Melt the butter in the same pan
  11. Fry the onions until they're soft while scraping browned bits stuck on the bottom of the pan
  12. Add garlic and ginger and sautee for one minute until fragrant, then add garam masala, cumin, turmeric and coriander
  13. Fry for about twenty seconds until fragrant, while stirring occasionally
  14. Pour in the tomato puree, chili powders and salt. Let simmer for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally until sauce thickens and becomes a deep brown red colour.
  15. Stir the cream and sugar through the sauce. Add the chicken and its juices back into the pan and cook for an additional 8-10 minutes until chicken is cooked through and the sauce is thick and bubbling. Pour in the water to thin out the sauce, if needed.
  16. Garnish with cilantro (coriander) and serve with hot garlic butter rice and fresh homemade Naan bread!
